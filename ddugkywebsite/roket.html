Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

Transforming Rural India through Skill Development

DDU-GKY is the flagship program of the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India, aimed at rural youth between the ages of 15 and 35 years.

Learn more

About DDU-GKY

DDU-GKY aims to provide market-oriented skills training and placement to rural youth in order to increase their employability and income levels. The program is implemented through Project Implementing Agencies (PIAs) and covers various sectors such as agriculture, healthcare, construction, and more.
